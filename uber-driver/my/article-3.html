A Whole New Experience Centered Around You

As an Uber driver, the first thing in your arsenal of resources is the Uber Driver App. Different from its Rider version, the Uber Driver App is an all-around tool that allows you to accept trip requests, navigate, know how much you have earned, and receive useful feedback. What You Can Do With The Uber App

Accept Rider Trip Requests

The Uber Driver App allows you to accept trip requests once you go online. You will get requests from nearby areas based on your geographical location. Your phone will beep and flash to notify you of a trip request; you can simply tap to accept.

Navigate Across The City

The Uber Driver App is your all-around tool that not only helps you accept trip requests but also helps you navigate! After accepting the trip request, the Uber Driver App will guide you to your rider’s location. They will then be notified once you are nearby. Once you pick up the rider, you will get directions to their destination.

Track How Much you Earned

After every trip, you can see how much you have earned for the day in comprehensive summaries. Earnings are automatically transferred to the bank account associated with your Uber profile every week.

Give and Receive Feedback

Uber Malaysia aims to develop a professional and respectful environment for both riders and drivers. Both parties will be able to rate each other after the trip has ended to ensure that they are safe and comfortable during the ride. If in the event that a driver rates a rider one (1) star, they will never paired with each other again.

How does an Uber Driver Earn

Perhaps one of the most important part of driving with Uber is getting your compensation. With the Uber Driver App, you will be able to track all your total earnings in thorough detail. It also helps you maximize and monetize your time on the road.

Track Your Earnings

You will get detailed weekly reports on exactly how much you have earned for the week so you would know if you had hit your goals.

Be at Popular Hotspots

Always be at the right place at the right time. The Uber Driver App shows you a live map where drivers are needed most.

Minimize Downtime

The Uber Driver App lets you accept trip requests when you approach your current rider’s destination. Subsequently, you can gear up for your next trip and maximize your time behind the wheel.

Make Money Fast

The Uber Driver App makes everything convenient for partners. Payments are made on a weekly basis electronically to your bank account. You can always access your payment statements online when you go to your driver dashboard.

As an Uber driver, you are always in control of your time, your earnings, and your career. Like an airline captain, you are the king of your own vehicle. Everything that you need to make the first trip is in your hands with the Uber Driver App in Malaysia!
