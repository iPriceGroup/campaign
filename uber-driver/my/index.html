"I enjoy driving Uber because of the flexibility. I can choose to start and stop working whenever I want to."

Ang SoonchoiCCTV Technician, KL

"With Uber, I gain something more than just extra pocket money. I was previously a shy person with no idea on how to start a conversation with a new person. After joining Uber, I gained more self-confidence and social skills to interact with others without hesitation. Thank you, Uber. I owe you something"

AinaSales Consultant, Gombak

"I like to drive with Uber because of the mysterious part of it. I wouldn’t know my drop off point and fare when I accept the order, it’s like playing a lucky draw when I start the trip. I always get surprised when I pick up the passenger. Surprise about who’s my rider, their destination and most of all, the conversation that I will have with them. It’s the perfect fit for someone who loves challenges like me."

WawaFull Time Driver, Cyberjaya

"I’m a property agent. Uber provides a platform for me to network with people from different backgrounds. Meeting prospective clients while earning extra money on the side, why not?"

LinaProperty Agent, KL & Puchong

AqiFull Time Driver, Kuala Lumpur

"The best part of driving is having the privilege to be on, literally, the front seat to hear stories and encounter random events you don’t get to experience on your daily job. I have met a lady who was going to, possibly, her final chemotherapy treatment and we talked about her regrets of not living her life as she always wanted to, I have met a young rider who just returned to KL from New York and I was the first person he told about his AIDS, I have gotten a ride request and got involved in a marriage proposal, and I also witnessed three different break-ups in my car in one day!! If you are open enough to serve strangers and create a bond beyond a one time driver-rider relationship, you can make a diverse group of friends from different parts of the world. That’s a truly magical experience and it all happens in a little space in your car"

AidanProject Manager & Part-time Driver
