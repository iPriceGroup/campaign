Why be an Uber Partner

Be in control of your schedule

With Uber, you can choose your own work schedule! Whether it’s during the day or the night, 7 days a week, 30 days a month; it’s all under your control. You can work four hours or eight hours, and can take as many or as little rides as you want! What job has that much flexibility?

The more you drive, the more you earn

All trips start with a base fare and may increase depending on the time and distance of the ride. When demand is higher, drivers can earn more! Basically, the more you drive, the more you earn!

Get all the support that you need from the Uber app!

Need help while you drive? Let the Uber app guide you. From navigation, fare calculation, Uber gives you access to tools to make your driving smoother.

What you Need to Start Driving

Who knew being your own boss is so easy? You don’t even need an SPM or a Master’s degree to start driving with Uber Malaysia! Most people can drive and earn cash using the app. To kickstart your career, here are some things that you need:

Minimum Driver Requirements

Even if you don’t have a commercial driver’s license, you can still sign up to be an Uber driver! Here are some basic requirements that you need to start driving.

  • Uber partners must be 21 years old and above.
  • Your Malaysian driver’s license.
  • No criminal record.

Vehicle Requirements

  • For drivers within Peninsular Malaysia, your vehicle should be no more than seven (7) years old.
  • In East Malaysia, your car should not be more than ten (10) years old.

For existing drivers who completed their profile before September 30, 2017, you can continue to drive with Uber until the maximum vehicle age of ten years.

Required Documents

Before you start driving with Uber Malaysia, you would need to upload the following documents when you sign up for the Uber Driver platform :

  • Your driver’s license.
  • MyKad or your IC.
  • An insurance cover note or proof of insurance (if  you’re using your own vehicle).

Begin your Journey with Uber Malaysia

With Uber, you can be an independent contractor, get paid weekly, and work on your own schedule. It’s a great way to earn extra cash without taking away your time for more important matters in your life! So, how exactly do you start driving with Uber?

Sign Up Online

First thing’s first, create your Uber driver account by sharing your personal details. Submit your name, your email, along with your phone number, and your address. There may also be vehicle requirements which may differ in every region.

Upload your Documents

After creating your profile, upload the needed documents such as a competent driver’s license, your IC or MyKad, an insurance cover note as proof of insurance if you’re using your own car, and other needed information.

Download the App and Start Driving!

Once everything is complete and your safety screening is cleared, you can download the Uber driver app as an independent contractor! You will have Uber to guide you 24/7 on how to navigate around the city and to guarantee overall rider satisfaction.

About the Uber Drive App

About to take your first step as an Uber partner? Don’t forget to download the Uber App for drivers! This app is carefully designed to cater to all your needs as an Uber partner. All you need to do is open the app and wait to receive trip requests from riders. Using the app, you can get all the information you need about the rider such as their name, their pickup location, and destination. Once the trip is over, you will instantly receive nearby requests. Finally, if you think you’re done for the day, simply sign off any time!
