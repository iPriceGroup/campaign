Uber Partner FAQs - What you Need to Know

Here are some questions that you may want to know the answer to when you drive with Uber Malaysia

Is it safe to drive with Uber?

Uber Malaysia walks the extra mile to provide partners with the utmost safety before, during, and after every ride. Once you go online using the Uber App, your location and route is tracked via GPS. Both drivers and riders need to verify specific information to confirm their identity. Uber partners can also decline or cancel a request. Furthermore, if you feel unsafe or whatever reason, Uber partners can choose to pull over and immediately end the trip. After every ride, you can also rate your rider and provide feedback. If you choose to rate a rider with one star (the lowest rating), you will not be matched with that rider again. You can read more about Uber rider safety.

What if the rider has multiple stops and changes destination?

Trips are calculated based on the total time and the distance of the trip. When the rider has multiple destinations or makes a stop mid-travel, the wait time in between is calculated within the total fare. Only swipe COMPLETE TRIP when the rider exits your vehicle at the destination.

Both partners and riders can change destinations at any time during the trip. To change the destination, simply enter the address on the destination bar on your Uber Driver App. As an Uber partner, it is a great habit to confirm the rider’s destination first before the ride or allow them to enter their preferred destinations.

When is it right to call the rider?

The Uber Driver app makes it easier for drivers to communicate with their riders. You, as the driver, will be able to send text messages or even call your rider if necessary. Both the driver and the rider’s numbers will be completely anonymous to keep both party’s privacy safe. To contact a rider, tap CONTACT then choose from the options available. You should also have a mobile device associated with your Uber account before you can get in touch with your rider.

It is good practice to call a rider during the following circumstances:

  • When you have waited more than a minute at their chosen pickup location.
  • When you are having trouble getting to the pickup location.
  • When you are unable to locate your rider.

As a safety precaution, both the rider and the Uber partner will be unable to contact each other using the Uber app after the trip ends.

Can I give rides to my friends and family when I’m online?

For the safety and comfort of Uber riders, friends and family may not be able to ride with you during a trip. It is only when you sign out of the Uber app can you allow other passengers in your vehicle.

I had a bad experience with a rider, what should I do?

Both riders and partners are expected to be professional and polite towards each other during a trip. You will be able to rate your rider so that we can create a better environment, not only for riders but also for partners. If you rate a rider with one (1) star, you will never partnered with that rider again. If you have further inquiries regarding a bad experience with a rider, you may get in touch with Uber Support.

Can I accept tips?

Uber Malaysia does not ask riders to tip their drivers. However, if a rider voluntarily offers gratuities, it is within the partner’s prerogative to accept.

What if I forget to start the trip on the Uber Driver App?

Fares are calculated based on the time and destination of the trip; from the moment you tap START TRIP to COMPLETE TRIP. Forgetting to log on to the Uber Driver App is a common mistake, even for seasoned drivers. If you feel like the start and end of a trip is incorrect, you can get in touch with Uber Support to make the necessary adjustments.

How long is the appropriate time to wait for an Uber Rider?

Ideally, once you accept a trip request, you should be able to pick up the rider on the location indicated on the Uber Driver App. When you are about one minute from the pickup point, rider’s will then be notified that you are nearby and will be arriving shortly. If you don’t see the rider or having trouble getting to the pickup location, you can get in touch with your rider via a phone call or a text message. Only start the trip once the rider gets in your car. You may inform the rider and politely cancel the trip request two (2) to five (5) minutes after you have arrived at the pickup location.

Pro tip: It’s great practice to ask your rider’s name to confirm that you are picking up the right Uber rider. Also, confirm the drop-off destination or any preferred route that the rider wishes to take.

What if a rider left something in my car?

It is uncommon for riders to leave their belongings inside an Uber partner’s vehicle. If you found an item that belongs to one of your riders, you can get in touch with Uber Support and share details of the item and the possible owner. Riders can also get in touch with their drivers in the event that an item is left behind in a partner’s vehicle. In the meantime, keep it safe until your rider gets in touch with you to recover the lost item. It is a great habit for Uber partners to remind their riders to take all their belongings when they exit your vehicle.

What if the rider makes a mess in my car?

Riders are expected to treat their Uber drivers with respect and professionalism. If you are in an incident where the rider caused damage to your vehicle beyond the normal wear and tear, you can get in touch with Uber Support. If the mess involves professional cleaning, you can always request a cleaning fee.

Should I be responsible for paying the toll fees?

Being a huge country, toll fees are common in Malaysia. Uber uses cutting-edge geographic technology that automatically adds toll fees to rider’s fare so you are immediately reimbursed for toll charges. It is always a great habit to ask your rider their preferred route, especially when it requires a toll.

What do I do if the Uber Rider App crashes while I’m on a trip?

Murphy’s law states that anything can go wrong will go wrong and that includes your Uber Driver app crashing while you are on a trip. If this is the case, you can try these steps to restart your Uber Driver App.

Force Quit

  1. Go to your home screen.
  2. Swipe the Uber App Card to force quit.
  3. Reopen the App.

Reset Network Settings

If issues still persist, you can always reset your network settings which can result with all your passwords being deleted. To do this, you need to do the following:

  1. Go to settings and select General.
  2. Tap on Reset and then Reset Network Settings
