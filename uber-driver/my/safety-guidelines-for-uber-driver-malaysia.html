Safety Guidelines for Uber Drivers in Malaysia

Your safety is always a priority

Utamakan keselamatan. that’s always the first thing to think about when you’re on the road. For your safety, your rider, and the driver’s around you, Uber is committed to providing their partners with all the tools they need before, during, and after the trip.

Sign up to drive

Before The Trip

No rider is anonymous

All riders are required to verify their account with their name and phone number before they can request a ride. When an Uber partner picks up on a trip request from the rider, you will know who you’ll be picking up.

During the Trip

Easy navigation

The Uber Driver app allows you to focus on getting riders from point A to point B by helping you navigate. Through the app, you will be able to get turn-by-turn directions once the rider enters the destination.

Always stay on the map

All GPS data is logged in every trip so we’ll know who you’re driving with and where you are.

After The Trip

No change? No worries!

Payments are charged automatically according to the rider’s preferred method of payment. Most riders enroll their credit/debit cards on their Uber account, so you don’t have to deal with change altogether!

Driver feedback

Both the driver and rider will be able to rate each other after every trip in the hopes of improving your experience with Uber. If a rider or a driver gives a one star rating, they will never be paired on another trip again.

24/7 support for drivers

Uber always has your back 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to help you with any questions that you may encounter while driving.

Uber will always be there for You

On the road, you will never know what could happen. Even the most responsible drivers get into accidents. Rest assured that Uber Malaysia has you covered even at these dire times so you can drive safely and worry-free.

Immediate Response

Uber has specially-trained response teams in Malaysia that would assist you in any incidents on the road ‘round the clock. When you drive with Uber, you are never alone on the road.

Assisting Local Law Enforcement

Uber cooperates with local law enforcement should the need arise. You can rest easy knowing that you are safe behind the wheel.

Be an Uber partner now and get the chance to become your own boss! For Uber, your safety is always a priority.
